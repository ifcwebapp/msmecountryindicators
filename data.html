Explore the data

Micro, Small and Medium Enterprise Country Indicators (MSME-CI) provides both the latest global snapshot and historic data back 20 years on the number of MSMEs in 132 world economies.

Please read the ‘Methodology Note on the MSME Country Indicators - 2014’ along with the country specific comments in the MSME-CI excel workbook before using the data. MSME-CI presents secondary data. The data are not always standardized across countries and time. The original data are collected by various institutions (statistical institutes, ministries, international organizations, small business promotion agencies, research institutions and others) using different methods (survey, census and others).

About the Data
Number of Economies: 132
Data Updates: 01/2007 - Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises: A Collection of Published Data
08/2010 - Micro, Small and Medium Enterprise Country Indicators (MSME-CI)
Format: Excel
MSME-CI Structure: 1. The most recent and complete data.
2. Historic data.
3. Estimated data on informal sector.
4. Data sources.
5. Detailed sector breakdown by economy.
Data Scope: MSME structure
  • MSME Definitions (number of employees, unless otherwise noted): micro, small and medium
  • Size Breakdown of the MSME Sector (% of all MSMEs): micro, small and medium
MSME Participation in the Economy
  • Number of micro, small and medium enterprises
  • MSME per 1000 people
  • MSME employment (% of total)
Distribution of MSMEs by Sectors
  • Percentage distribution of micro, small and medium enterprises in manufacturing, trade, services, agriculture/other
Sources: National institutes of statistics; respective ministries, such as economy, labor and others; respective governmental agencies, such as company registers and MSME promotion agencies; international organizations.
